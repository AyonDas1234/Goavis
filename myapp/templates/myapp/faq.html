Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I book a delivery?
You can do the booking by using the online form on our website.

2. How early should I book a courier?
As soon as your order is confirmed you can do the booking. To be in safe place you can do the booking 30 mins in advance.

3. Do you pack the goods?
No we don’t pack the goods, but instead we have bags where we put in the packages.

4. What do I do if I want to send several items to the recipient?
On the form, add your drop point by clicking “Add location”.

5. Do you offer Baggages for transport?
Yes, we offer bags as per the requirement of the package that is to be delivered. We have bags specially for temperature-controlled cold chain, cake, bakery, other food materials, flowers, high perishable goods.

6. How does WE help businesses with their delivery service?
WE provides logistics solutions for businesses by providing bikes on demand for business parcel delivery, bulk courier services for small and medium businesses.

7. Does WE provide logistics service for both B2B and B2C deliveries?
Yes, WE provides logistic & delivery services for both B2B & B2C sectors. Just book OUR bike and deliver commercial goods or household items to business customers, wholesalers, retailers, distributors, shop owners, or personal customers.

8. What is the minimum charge for your courier?
We charge flat 10/- per km. Our base fare is at 50/- for a delivery up to 5 kms. After that, 10/- per km will be added till drop locations.

9. Do you have surge pricing?
No, we don’t have any surge pricing at any conditions. We charge a flat 10/- per km from pick up to drop locations.

10. Do you have midnight delivery?
Yes, we have midnight delivery. Our charges for midnight delivery start at 20/- per km.

11. What kinds of items can I deliver with YOU?
Anything & everything apart from restricted & illegal items, you can deliver all items up to 20kg that can be carried on a bike using US.

12. Do you deliver Cake, Bakery items?
Yes, we deliver all types of cakes & bakery items made by home bakers, bakery businesses. We have specialized cold chain bags for delivery of all types of cakes. We deliver cream cakes, fondant cakes, tier cakes, and all others.

13. Do you deliver bulk food deliveries from home chefs, cloud kitchens, restaurants?
Yes, we deliver bulk orders of our partners. Presently we can do delivery of food up to 100 persons. For this, we charge 5/- per plate above 3 plates.

14. Are there any charges for using US for Business?
There are no charges to sign up on US for Business. You will be only charged for the deliveries you make on US for business.

15. Do you do Cash on Delivery collection for your customers?
Yes, we do COD collection on delivery of the items. We transfer the COD in 1 day time.

16. Do you refund if a cake is damaged while doing delivery?
No, presently we don’t refund “IF” any cake is damaged while on delivery. We try to make sure that the cake is not damaged while doing delivery.

17. Do you have insurance for the packages that are not delivered?
No, presently we do not have any insurance policy.

18. Where is US available?
Today, WE are available only in Kolkata. Presently we are doing pick-ups from only South Kolkata, but we are delivering all across Kolkata. Presently, our riders are based in only South Kolkata.
